Whiplash

2014 🥁 Rated R 🥁 Drama 🥁 Music 🥁 1 Hour 46 Minutes

A Damien Chazelle Film

Starring Miles Teller, J.K. Simmons, Paul Reiser, Melissa Benoist, Austin Stowell, and Nate Lang

My Review: "10/10. Whiplash is perfection. Miles Teller, and J.K. Simmons brought a movie experience I've never felt before. The plot was simple but so god damn affective. The cinematography with beautiful. The pacing was just right to keep me at the edge of my seat. The drama and music was everything I could've asked for. Andrew is a protagonist that has faults but is till so loveable. Terrence Fletcher is the best performed character I've ever seen. The ending was so bitter sweet and not overdone. Everything is perfect."
