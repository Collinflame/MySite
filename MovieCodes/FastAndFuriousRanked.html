Worst to Best

The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift

A Justin Lin Film

Starring Lucas Black, Bow Wow, Sung Kang, Brian Tee, Nathalie Kelley, Sonny Chiba, and Brian Goodman

My Review: "7.5/10. The races are progressing and everything else is regressing."

Fast X

2023 🏎 Rated PG-13 🏎 Action 🏎 Adventure 🏎 2 Hours 21 Minutes

A Louis Leterrier Film

Starring Vin Diesel, Jason Momoa, Michelle Rodriguez, John Cena, Tyrese Gibson, Chris Bridges, Sung Kang, Nathalie Emmanuel, Brie Larson, Daniela Melchior, Charlize Theron, Alan Ritchson, and Jason Statham

My Review: "7.5/10. This is just Avengers: Infinity War. It's the first part of a grand finale of an epic (or for F&F not so epic) saga. It has the villain that has been plotting since a very early movie and has a lot of scenes that should've killed the characters. However, unlike Infinity War, these characters don't have powers or futuristic gear. Instead, they only survive because of faith and family. They seemingly killed half the crew but considering Gisele is not really dead I'm just going to assume they all lived. The plot is absolutely ridiculous with a crazy amount of plot holes. The only great part about it is Jason Momoa as Dante. I was hoping that they will raise the stakes and the quality. Instead, they only raised the stakes."
